Rishi Bharat

Discover India's Greatest Sages & Scholars

Rishi Bharat is an educational game for kids to learn about India's legendary rishis, sages, and scholars through quizzes, interactive stories, comics, AI-powered adventures, and collectible cards.

Features

  • Rishis Encyclopedia — Explore 20 sages with search, filters, bios, fun facts, and special abilities
  • Quiz Mode — 120 questions across 20 rishis with 3 difficulty levels
  • Story Mode — 20 branching adventures where your choices matter
  • AI Story Generator — On-device AI creates unique adventures for each rishi
  • Comic Reader — Visual comic stories for 6 legendary rishis
  • Card Collection — Collect all 20 rishi cards with stats and facts
  • Adaptive Difficulty — Automatically adjusts to your child's level
  • Gamification — Points, levels, streaks, and unlockables

FAQ

  • Is the app free? — Yes, Rishi Bharat is completely free.
  • Does it require internet? — No, the app works fully offline. AI Stories require Apple Intelligence on supported devices.
  • What age group is it for? — Designed for kids aged 5-10.
  • Does it contain ads? — No, the app is completely ad-free.
  • How do I unlock cards? — Score 80%+ on a quiz or complete a rishi's story.
  • What are the 20 rishis? — Valmiki, Vyasa, Vishwamitra, Vashistha, Agastya, Narada, Atri, Bharadwaja, Kapila, Kanada, Patanjali, Panini, Baudhayana, Aryabhata, Charaka, Sushruta, Chanakya, Kalidasa, Thiruvalluvar, and Ramanujacharya.
