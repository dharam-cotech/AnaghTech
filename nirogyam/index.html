Nirogyam

Your Complete Ayurveda Guide

Nirogyam (निरोग्यम् — "The Disease-Free State") is a complete Ayurveda guide app that helps you align your daily routines, diet, and wellness practices with ancient Ayurvedic wisdom. Discover seasonal regimens, kitchen remedies, body constitution analysis, and more — all completely offline and private.

Features

  • Dinacharya — Daily routines aligned with Ayurvedic principles
  • Ahara (Kitchen Pharmacy) — 20 spices and 30 remedies from your kitchen
  • Prakriti — Dosha Quiz and Body Map to understand your constitution
  • Nirogyam (Seasonal Living) — Seasonal regimens for balanced wellness
  • Chikitsa — Gharelu Remedies, Saundarya Vigyan, Panchagavya, Mudra, and Nabhi therapies
  • Ayushcharya Calendar — Hindu dates, Nakshatra plants, and Rashi guides
  • AI Vaidya Companion — On-device AI powered by Apple FoundationModels, fully private

FAQ

  • Is the app free? — Yes, Nirogyam is completely free. No subscriptions, no in-app purchases.
  • Does it work offline? — Yes, 100% offline. No internet connection needed.
  • Is it safe for children? — Yes, Nirogyam is family-friendly and safe for all ages.
  • Does it show ads? — No, never. The app is completely ad-free.
  • Is the AI private? — Yes, all AI processing happens on your device using Apple FoundationModels. No data is ever sent to any server.
