Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Overview

Nirogyam (निरोग्यम् — "Your Complete Ayurveda Guide") is a comprehensive Ayurveda guide app designed for users of all ages, including families with children. We take your privacy extremely seriously. This policy explains how we handle data in our app.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. Nirogyam does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information from its users, including children.

What Data Is Stored

The app stores your preferences and progress locally on your device only. This includes dosha quiz results, habit garden progress, and user profiles. This data:

  • Is stored only on your device using standard iOS storage
  • Is never transmitted to any server or third party
  • Is never shared with anyone
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the app

Internet & Network

Nirogyam does not require an internet connection and does not make any network requests. The app works completely offline.

On-Device AI

Nirogyam uses Apple FoundationModels for its AI Vaidya companion feature. All AI processing happens entirely on your device. No prompts, queries, or responses are ever sent to any external server. Your interactions with the AI assistant remain completely private.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no ads in the app. There are no subscriptions or in-app purchases.

Children's Privacy

Nirogyam is designed to be safe for users of all ages, including children. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or any other users.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the changes on this page with an updated date.
