Veer Bharat

Learn About India's Greatest Heroes

Veer Bharat is an educational game for kids to learn about India's legendary warriors and kings through quizzes, interactive stories, and collectible cards.

Features

  • Quiz Mode — 46 questions across 8 warriors
  • Story Mode — Branching adventures where your choices matter
  • Card Collection — Collect all 8 warrior cards with stats and facts
  • Gamification — Points, levels, streaks, and unlockables

FAQ

  • Is the app free? — Yes, Veer Bharat is completely free.
  • Does it require internet? — No, the app works fully offline.
  • What age group is it for? — Designed for kids aged 5-10.
  • Does it contain ads? — No, the app is ad-free.
  • How do I unlock cards? — Score 80%+ on a quiz or complete a warrior's story.
