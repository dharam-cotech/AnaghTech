Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Overview

Veer Bharat ("Warriors of Bharat") is an educational game designed for children. We take your privacy and your child's privacy very seriously. This policy explains how we handle data in our app.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. Veer Bharat does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information from its users, including children.

What Data Is Stored

The app stores game progress (points, levels, completed quizzes, unlocked cards) locally on your device only. This data:

  • Is stored only on your device using standard iOS/Android storage
  • Is never transmitted to any server or third party
  • Is never shared with anyone
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the app

Internet & Network

Veer Bharat does not require an internet connection and does not make any network requests. The app works completely offline on both iOS and Android.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no ads in the app.

Children's Privacy

Veer Bharat is designed for children aged 5-10. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the changes on this page with an updated date.
